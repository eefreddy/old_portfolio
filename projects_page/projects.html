Projects and Activities

Capstone Project- Wearable Biomedical Sensing Device

My team and I built this device in partnership with our client Clinisys as part of the ECE 490/491 course. This device measures wearer's heart rate, blood oxidation, skin temperature, ambient temperature and orientation and all the data is transmitter to a host device.

We were awarded the "Ronald E & Jackie Philips Award" by the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering at the University of Alberta for our "superior academic achievement and overall excellence in design project management". We were also nominated for the Eleven Engineering Prize.

Personal Project- Random Jingle Generator

Spawned from a particularly interesting lab where I implemented Conway's "Game of Life" using rule 30 on an FPGA board. I coded this in Python using my knowledge of music. It creates a random jingle (musically coherent) depending on which root note you pick!
